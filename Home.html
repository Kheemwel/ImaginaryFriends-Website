Imaginary Friends

You are the child in this supernatural visual novel. You must survive and keep your sanity and happiness. For you to keep it, you must talk and befriend your new imaginary friends. But, be warned, not everything is what it seems. Now, can you survive and keep your sanity and happiness? Will you make the right choices and have a happy ending? Download the game now to find out!

Plot

The protagonist, an 8-year-old child, just moved into their new house. The child suffers from a verbally abusive mother and a neglectful father, living with many pains, anxiety, and depression. Suffering from many hardships at a young age, the child’s sanity and happiness is at risk. Thankfully though, the child met many imaginary friends that will help the child be happier and sane, but be careful, not everything is what it seems.
